Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service.

  • 1. What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and local bank transfers. We are also planning to introduce support for cryptocurrencies in the near future.

  • 2. How long does shipping usually take?

    Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days within the United States. International shipping times vary between 10-20 business days depending on the destination and local customs processing.

  • 3. What is your return policy?

    We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products, provided they are returned in their original condition with all packaging intact. Please contact our support team to initiate a return process.

  • 4. Can I change or cancel my order after it has been placed?

    If your order has not yet been processed for shipping, we can usually accommodate cancellations or modifications. Please call us immediately at `1-800-GEMINI` with your order number to request changes.

  • 5. Do you offer technical support for your software products?

    Yes, complimentary technical support is included with every purchase. Our team is available 24/7 via live chat and email to assist with installation, troubleshooting, and general inquiries.
